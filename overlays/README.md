# Overlay Video Clips Library

This directory holds categorized overlay video clips (B-roll, film grain, light leaks, glitch effects, blend transitions) that can be loaded and layered into video projects.

## Categories:
- `light-leaks/`: Vintage & modern light leak overlay videos
- `film-grain/`: 16mm/35mm cinematic film grain overlay loops
- `glitch/`: Digital distortion & VHS glitch transition clips
- `transitions/`: Wipe, flash, and motion blend transition overlays
