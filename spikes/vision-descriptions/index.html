Spike — can Claude describe a clip from sampled frames?

Tests ADR-0001. Grabs a few frames from each clip (via <video> + <canvas>, downscaled), sends them to a Claude vision model, and shows the returned Clip Description. Read the descriptions: are they distinct and concrete enough that a downstream editor could sequence a story from them alone?
